Flood Preparedness

Floods can cause significant damage and disruption. Taking proactive steps can help minimize risk and ensure safety.

  • Know your flood risk and evacuation routes.
  • Assemble an emergency kit.
  • Disconnect electrical appliances.
  • Move valuables to higher ground.

Heatwave Preparedness

Heatwaves are periods of extremely hot weather that can be dangerous. Knowing how to prepare can help you stay healthy and safe.

  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.
  • Avoid outdoor activities in the sun.
  • Wear light-colored clothing.
  • Find a cool place to rest.
